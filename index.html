How to Play

Why Daily Typing Challenge?

Build Muscle Memory & Speed

Consistent daily practice is the key to mastering touch typing. Our algorithm selects unique sentences every day to prevent rote memorization and ensure you are truly reading and processing text.

Multilingual Proficiency

By typing quotes in English, Korean, Japanese, Spanish, and French, you not only improve your WPM but also familiarize yourself with diverse sentence structures and vocabulary naturally.

Scientific Ranking System

Unlike simple average scores, our ranking is based on a Gaussian Normal Distribution of global typing speeds. Your "Top %" reflects your true standing among typists worldwide, providing a realistic goal for improvement.
